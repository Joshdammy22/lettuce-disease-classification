Common Pests and Diseases

Lettuce can be affected by various pests and diseases such as aphids and downy mildew. Regular monitoring and appropriate treatments can help manage and prevent these issues effectively.

Crop Rotation Benefits

Implementing crop rotation by alternating lettuce with other crops can help reduce the buildup of pests and diseases, improve soil health, and enhance overall crop yield.

Lettuce Facts

Interesting Statistics

Lettuce is one of the most widely consumed leafy greens in the world. It's known for its high water content and nutritional value. Did you know? Lettuce can be grown year-round in controlled environments, making it a versatile crop for many farmers.

Nutritional Information

Lettuce is low in calories and high in vitamins and minerals. It contains essential nutrients like Vitamin A, Vitamin K, and folate, which are important for maintaining overall health and wellness.
